Token Economics

Why a token?

A token has two primary purposes in Blender: (i) to decentralise the governance process, and (ii) to attract non-privacy aware users. By attracting different user demographics (namely privacy-sensitive users, and users interested in the token), the anonymity set of the mixer is likely to experience a more diverse and bigger set of users. This will then hopefully improve the privacy of all involved users, and hence provide a strong utility to the entire Ethereum ecosystem.

BLND Token Structure

BLND is an ERC-20 token, which serves as governance token for Blender. The governance process is envisioned to vote on important system parameters, such as:

  • Voting on which new pools should be added to Blender.
  • Voting on the time parameter, which decides on how long a deposit needs to remain in Blender to become eligible for a token reward.
  • Voting on other technical changes that might improve Blender (such as the choice of hash function).
  • Addition/Removal of relayer (those that help paying for gas of the withdrawal and reward transactions).

Token Allocation

The total token supply amounts to 420 Million BLND. 200M BLND are divided equally among the 4 ETH pools (50M each), while 100M BLND are kept as a reserve that can be used by the DAO, for example to incentivise future pools. 120M BLND tokens are available to early contributors of Blender. BLND is allocated on a first come first serve basis, following the below numbers per mixer pool.

BLND for each pool
32,436.36First 1,000 rewards per pool
200For the subsequent 10,000 rewards per pool
15For the next 1,037,576 rewards per pool

Every pool (1, 10, 100 and 1000 ETH) can mint at most 50M BLND tokens. For example, if you perform a deposit into the 1000 ETH pool, wait 5 days, and then request a reward, you are allocated 32,436.36 BLND, if you are one of the first 1,000 depositors in doing so (and if there’s at least one deposit on your 5th day). This mechanism incentivises users to join a new pool, in an attempt to increase the anonymity set, and hence improve the overall privacy provided by the pool. Note that only the first 1,000 reward requests receive the highest reward, the contracts follow the first come first serve principle (FCFS).

The DAO is planned as an instrument to vote for example on new pools. Note that the 0.1 ETH pool does not grant any BLND rewards.

Where to purchase BLND?

There’s no need to purchase BLND, please deposit coins into BLND to receive a chance to better privacy plus a BLND reward.
